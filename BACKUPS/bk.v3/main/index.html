Contribute to Pythons and Ladders

Pythons and Ladders is free to use, and we intend for it to remain so.

However, if you feel that Pythons and Ladders has added value to your life in any meaningful way, please consider contributing to its continued development and presence on the web.

With your support, we hope to initiate many more students into the world of programming, many of whom cannot afford to pay for a learning experience.

Thank you for your kindness.
