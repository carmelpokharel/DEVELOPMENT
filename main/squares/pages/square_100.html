Task One Hundred

Define a function, solve(), whose input is a str with a mathematical expression, and output is its solution.

Your function will be evaluated on the following expressions:

7 – 4 + 6 × 8 ÷ 2

14 + 8 (6 ÷ 0)

(5 – 1) (4 + 7)

102 – 10 + 32

30 – 3 (4.5 × 2)2 + 1

The expressions will be passed to solve() in the following formats:

'7 - 4 + 6 * 8 / 2'

'14 + 8(6 / 0)'

'(5 - 1)(4 + 7)'

'10^2 - 10 + 3^2'

'30 - 3(4.5 * 2)^(2 + 1)'
